Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

TubeMentor ("we", "our", or "us") operates the TubeMentor iOS application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • YouTube URLs you submit for analysis purposes
  • Video metadata fetched via the YouTube Data API v3 (title, description, tags, view count, like count)
  • Usage data such as number of analyses performed, stored locally on your device
  • Purchase information processed by Apple's App Store (we do not store payment details)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide AI-powered video analysis using the Anthropic Claude API
  • Track your monthly usage quota for freemium plan enforcement
  • Store your analysis history locally on your device
  • Process subscription purchases through Apple StoreKit

4. Third-Party Services

TubeMentor uses the following third-party services:

  • YouTube Data API v3 (Google) — to fetch public video metadata. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API — to power AI analysis. Video metadata is sent to Anthropic for processing. Governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — to process in-app purchases. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage

All your analysis history and preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. We do not maintain a server-side database of your personal data or analysis history.

6. Data Retention

Your local data is retained until you delete the app or clear app data. You can delete your analysis history at any time from the app's Settings screen.

7. Children's Privacy

TubeMentor is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

Frequently Asked Questions

How does video analysis work?

TubeMentor fetches publicly available metadata from any YouTube video (title, description, tags, view count, likes) using the YouTube Data API, then sends this data to our AI engine to generate a detailed analysis and score.

Does TubeMentor access my YouTube account?

No. TubeMentor only reads public video data. We never ask for your YouTube login credentials or access your private channel data.

Why is my analysis count not resetting?

Your monthly quota resets on the 1st of each month. If you're having issues, go to Settings and check your reset date. Contact support if the problem persists.

Can I analyze private or unlisted videos?

No. TubeMentor can only analyze publicly available YouTube videos.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone Settings → tap your Apple ID → Subscriptions → TubeMentor → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

The analysis seems inaccurate. Why?

AI-generated analysis is based on publicly available metadata and general YouTube best practices. Results are meant as guidance, not guarantees. If you believe a specific analysis is significantly wrong, please contact us with details.
