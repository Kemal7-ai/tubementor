3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Providing core app functionality: Analyzing YouTube videos and generating AI-powered insights
  • Quota management: Tracking monthly analysis usage to enforce subscription tiers fairly
  • Displaying history: Showing you your past analyses stored locally on your device
  • Subscription management: Determining your access level (Free, Starter, Pro, Creator) via Apple StoreKit

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, marketing, or any purpose beyond those listed above.

The legal basis for processing under GDPR is: performance of a contract (Article 6(1)(b)) — processing is necessary to provide the service you have requested.

4. Third-Party Services

TubeMentor relies on the following third-party services to function. Each service processes data under its own privacy policy:

4.1 YouTube Data API v3 (Google LLC)

We use YouTube's official API to fetch publicly available video metadata (title, description, tags, statistics). No private or account data is accessed. This service is governed by:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • YouTube API Terms of Service

Data sent: YouTube video URL. Data received: public video metadata only.

4.2 Anthropic Claude API (Anthropic, PBC)

We use Anthropic's Claude AI to analyze video metadata and generate insights. The video metadata (title, description, tags, statistics) is sent to Anthropic's servers for processing. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests. This service is governed by:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Usage Policy

Data sent to Anthropic: Video title, description, tags, view count, like count, comment count, duration. No user identifiers are sent.

4.3 Apple StoreKit (Apple Inc.)

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information. This service is governed by:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Apple Media Services Terms

No other third-party services are used. TubeMentor does not include advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, crash reporting tools, or social media integrations.

5. Data Storage & Security

5.1 Local Storage

All your analysis history, usage counts, and app preferences are stored exclusively on your device using iOS UserDefaults and local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.

5.2 No Server-Side Storage

TubeMentor does not operate its own backend servers or databases. We do not store any user data on remote servers controlled by us.

5.3 Security Measures

  • All API communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • API keys are stored securely within the application bundle
  • Local data is protected by iOS device encryption (when device passcode is enabled)
  • We do not transmit sensitive personal data over unencrypted channels

5.4 Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting user data, we will notify affected users and relevant supervisory authorities within the timeframes required by applicable law (72 hours under GDPR, promptly under KVKK).

6. Data Retention

Since data is stored locally on your device, you control retention:

  • Analysis history: Retained until you delete the app or manually clear history in Settings
  • Usage counters: Reset monthly automatically; deleted when app is uninstalled
  • Subscription status: Managed by Apple; restorable via "Restore Purchases"

Data sent to Anthropic (Claude API) for processing is subject to Anthropic's own retention policies. Based on Anthropic's published policy, API inputs and outputs are not used to train models without explicit consent.

Data sent to Google (YouTube API) is subject to Google's own retention policies as described in their privacy policy.
