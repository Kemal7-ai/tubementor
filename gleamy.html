Last updated: March 24, 2026
01

Overview

Gleamy ("we", "us", or "our") is a wellness application that uses your device's front-facing camera to measure smile intensity. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

✦ We never store images of your face. All face tracking happens entirely on your device using Apple's ARKit framework.
02

Camera & Face Data

Gleamy uses Apple's ARKit TrueDepth camera technology to measure your smile. Here's what actually happens:

  • ARKit projects 30,000 infrared points onto your face and processes this data entirely on-device using Apple's Neural Engine.
  • Gleamy only receives two numeric values: mouthSmileLeft and mouthSmileRight (floating point numbers between 0.0 and 1.0).
  • No images, no video, no 3D facial maps are ever stored or transmitted.
  • Face data never leaves your device.
✦ Because we only process two anonymous numeric values on-device, Gleamy does not collect biometric identifiers as defined under GDPR, KVKK, or CCPA.
03

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Gleamy is free and supported by ads. We use Google AdMob to display interstitial advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, if you grant permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage data for ad targeting
  • Device type, OS version, and language

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time in your device's Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

✦ When submitting to the App Store, we declare "Third-Party Advertising" data use in our App Privacy section, in compliance with Apple's requirements.
04

In-App Purchases

Gleamy offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple.

05

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration required — no name, email, or password
  • No photos or videos of your face
  • No biometric identifiers
  • No precise location data
  • No data is shared with third parties beyond AdMob
06

Your Rights & Data Deletion

Since Gleamy does not collect personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to delete. However, for advertising-related data collected by AdMob:

  • Reset your Advertising ID: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Reset Advertising Identifier
  • Opt out of personalized ads: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → turn off Personalized Ads
  • Request Google data deletion: myaccount.google.com
